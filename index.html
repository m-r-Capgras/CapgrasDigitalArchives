ABOUT

The Archives

The Capgras Archives were created and is maintained by the independant paranormal investigator Mr. Capgras (alias), for the purposes of documenting and sharing his discoveries and investigations.
Specializing in digital mediums, Mr. Capgras often investigates phenomenon that require digging deeper and direct contact. All findings are kept in the archives for ease of access and for the sake of preservation.

Mr. Capgras

Interested in the paranormal and the technicals of computers from a young age, Mr. Capgras has always sought out to dig out unseen mysteries from his favorite media. Especially in video games.
With his unquenching love for mystique and exploring the unknown, he would often surf the internet about mysterious webpages, creepypastas, digital phenomena and horror related content.
He liked to listen to stories about supposedly cursed games, one about an abandoned FPS that housed a ghost, one about a visual novel with sentient characters, one about 'A Dark Place'.
As a pre-teen, he would come across such a game himself for the first time. Found it in the bio of someone named "Ivan Zanotti" in a popular game sharing platform, it had no real title. The description read "I need a heart to open it"
The next 4 hours of his life were both some of the scariest and most intriguing moments of his short existance. Ever since then it wasn't just about listening to stories and findings from other mouths. He would actively seek out and explore such mysteries himself.
That came to a halt beyond the age of 13 for personal reasons, however. Only recently was he able to meaningfully return to this passion of his. With the INTERLOPER mystery and the Cogware tragedy rekindling his interest.
This website is an attempt to fuel this reviven torch and to advance it further than just a casual hobby.

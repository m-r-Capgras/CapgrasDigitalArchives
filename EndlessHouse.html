Description

Endless House is a dangerous, Labrinthine type Layer 2 dimension accessible from Wonderland[D] and Winter Wonderland. The entrance is a house made out of oak with a sealed off room containing a black portal in Wonderland[D] and a small oak hut with a black portal inside in Winter Wonderland. This dimension is a big, infinitely stretching, 3 story, maze-like house made mostly out of oak. It consists of surreal rooms vaguely resembling what one might find in an average player made base structure in Minecraft. All rooms are interconnected, and most walls are no thicker than a single block. Occasionally, travelers can find big centralized passageways connecting to hallways. These can be used to make traversal easier. As most other passages are narrow and claustrophobic. Staircases and ladders may also be found, letting travelers access into the upper or lower floors. It is recommended to have an axe readily available. It is vital that one must always be on the move, even more than other Labrinthine type dimensions. One of the entities that can be found here is unoficially called the Sentinel. It is a humanoid entity with pitch black skin and only a single, giant eye covering it's face. It will constantly and slowly pursue anyone within the Endless House. Never stopping. Alongside the Sentinel, other dangerous entities can also be found here. Most of them originating from Layer 1 dimensions. One notable example is the Eyeless One from the Brickways. It is not known how they find their way into the house, but it is believed Wonderland itself positions them there. In order to exit, one must simply find a portal. There are three known ones. Two of them are not documented. A white portal found in an empty room will take one back to the Overworld. This is currently the only known way to get out of the Wonderland alive.

Anomaly Description

Wonderland is a viral, software based digital anomaly that clings itself in copies of the video game Minecraft.
It stays dormant until someone opens the infected program on the device. Which then it invades the regular gameplay of the player in attempts to observe or interact with them.
During runtime, sentient abnormalities forming as NPC mobs will attempt to transport the player within the anomaly's confines in an in-game set of dimensions.
While there is no recorded evidence of players experiencing harm, the anomaly does seem to have a victim count. These victims are theorized to now be residing withing these afformentioned dimensions.

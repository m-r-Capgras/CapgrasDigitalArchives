Description

Winter Wonderland is a risky, Plane type Layer 2 dimension accessible from Wonderland[D]. The only known method of reaching here is by sleeping in Wonderland[D]. Upon doing so, the person sleeping will immediatly be sent here. If you do not wish to transport yourself here, make sure the day-night cycle is not in it's "night" state when you activate a bed. The landscape is barren with constant snowfall. The ground is a thick layer of snow with nothing but bedrock underneath it. Structures are scarce, with most not containing anything of common interest. One notable structure is a decorated taiga tree with chests underneath it containing a random single item. It resembles what one may interpret as a Christmas tree. On top is a torch with a single white portal block on top of it. Upon touching it, however. It will dissapear. Alongside it, the Lankman will appear to give chase to whoever touched the block. There are no other known entities, but there have been reports of the Sentinel communicating with travelers. There are two known ways to exit here. One is to fall into a pack of powder snow. Where it leads is currently unknown. The other is to find a small oak hut with a black portal inside. This will take you to the Endless House. Overall this is a dimension shrouded in mystery. Avoiding it is recommended.
